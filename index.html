Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel?

Cancel anytime. On iPhone: device Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Cheffy → Cancel. On Android: Play Store app → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Cheffy → Cancel. Your premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Cheffy is built local-first — your meal plan, saved recipes, and shopping list are stored on your device and available without signal. Only AI features (creating recipes from text, scanning photos, importing from URLs) require an internet connection. When you're back online, your plan syncs across your devices automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists belong to you and aren't sold or shared. We use standard processors (Supabase for storage, PostHog for anonymous analytics, RevenueCat for subscription management) under contract to operate the app. Full details in our Privacy Policy.

Can I share with family?

Not yet — Cheffy is single-user today. Shared meal planning for households is one of the most-requested features, and it's on the roadmap. Email me and I'll put you on the list to notify when it ships.

How accurate are AI recipes?

Most of the time, very. The AI pulls from tested recipe structures and preserves ingredients, quantities, and steps when you import from the web or scan from a photo. That said, AI isn't perfect — occasionally an ingredient gets misread or a step is off. If you hit a bad recipe, let me know — the more reports I get, the better the model gets.
